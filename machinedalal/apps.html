Why Machine Dalal?

Machine Dalal is a foremost online platform for selling and buying print, packaging and converting machinery worldwide. All print machinery manufacturing companies as well as individuals can register on the platform and list their equipment to reach more buyers globally and sell. You can also search for the equipment that meets your requirements from our wide range of categories and buy directly from the seller without any interference. You can learn more here.

How can I register?

Sign up with your e-mail address or you can simply register with your phone number/Google account/Apple Id. For more information tap here.

How do I list machines?

To list a machine that you want to sell on Machine Dalal, first you need to register and then add your machine from your account. Enter manufacturer, model and year of manufacturing along with a detailed description of the equipment that is being listed. Specify the location and do upload equipment images from different angles as well as a video. Tap here to know more.

Why should I provide a detailed description when listing machine?

A detailed description and all the technical specifications related to your machine along with multiple images as well as video ensure maximum exposure for your machine and better the chances of selling quickly. In addition, it provides a general idea to the prospects regarding machine’s condition and operational capacity.

How can I look for machines?

You can find the machines by searching for them according to their categories and subcategories or simply use our internal search engine. You can further refine your search by applying product specific filters that make the search for machines smoother and straightforward.

How can I contact the seller?

To contact the seller, first you need to register with Machine Dalal. You can then contact the seller either through his personal email/phone number or our in-built messaging service. All the conversations taking place between you and the seller using our in-built messaging can be accessed from ‘Conversation’ on main menu. Learn more here.
