Manroland-A Fine Example of German Engineering

The firm centres on the philosophy of R&D and innovation

Manroland Sheetfed GmbH is one of the top global producers of sheetfed offset printing presses and related technologies for commercial, packaging, and publishing applications.

Founded in 1871 and based upon the finest traditions of German engineering excellence, the company is a global watchword for quality, precision, and supreme reliability. Every press they deliver is designed and built in Offenbach, Germany.

Company philosophy

Since its inception, Manroland Sheetfed has adhered to the philosophy that R & D is the key to creating the next generation of advanced print technologies. With this in mind, the company has continually focused its efforts on research and technological development, making the strengthening of basic research and fundamental technologies an important management issue, and has proceeded to create new technologies and technological areas.

Innovation at its core

Besides, Manroland clearly distinguishes between its research activities and development activities, placing priority on highly innovative basic and exploratory research.

The Print Technology Centre

The Print Technology Centre (PTC) in Offenbach provides ideal conditions to become acquainted with the best-in-class technology in sheetfed offset printing and to put their systems to the test. From development to marketability, the PTC offers a comprehensive spectrum of interdisciplinary services: mechanics, electronics, process engineering, and extensive know-how in prepress and printing.

Remote service

Also, depending on the printing system, the failure is reported to their service technicians who are reachable 24X7 at the click of a button or through the telephone.

When the message is received, a service technician promptly carries out a remote diagnosis and liaises with the operating personnel to decide the measures to be taken. What’s best? Most problems can be resolved by remote support which reduces downtimes and avoids travel costs. When you invest in a Manroland press, you invest good.
