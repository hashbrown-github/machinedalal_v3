ROLAND 700 EVOLUTION Elite

Giving printers unprecedented levels of efficiency and productivity

The ROLAND 700 EVOLUTION Elite with its sleek, futuristic look, is the thoroughbred of 3B and 3B Plus presses for packaging and commercial applications.

Replete with new technological features, it gives printers unparalleled levels of output and quality. With its global benchmarking in inking unit technology and ecological advancements with its innovative drive and bearing technology, the Elite is a cut above other 18,000 sph presses.

Its features

  • InlineColorPilot 3.0

  • InlineInspector 3.0

  • new PressPilot

  • IntegrationPilot plus.

  • IntegrationPilot plus.

  • Plate changing systems and Autoprint are just some of its features

Other specifications include:

Ergonomic operating user interface with touch-screens
Direct Drive technology
Total efficiency at 18,000 sph
Feeder, delivery and dampening units, specially designed for high-speed production
Unique features include TripleFlow inking unit, intelligent speed compensation for inking and dampening units, effective anti-ghosting solutions
Optional 3B Plus 780 x 1,050 mm sheet size for maximum multiple-up images

Exceptional performance

Due to Manroland’s simultaneous automation, core make-ready steps such as plate exchange, blanket, and impression cylinder wash-up are simultaneous. The make-ready function in AutoPrint realizes production changeover in under 4 minutes.

Manroland’s DirectDrive principle further automates all press processes delivering a staggering reduction of up to 60% in make-ready time, including the exclusive ability to change print length, on the fly, making the ROLAND 700 Evolution Elite the ideal choice in the 70/100 format for short production runs.

Premium print quality

Even after several hundred million impressions, users are continually impressed by the finishes achieved.

The TripleFlow inker delivers more control than any other comparable technology on the market. With three different ink distributions at the push of a button, it offers high-colour consistency during the whole print run and leads to drastic savings in waste.

The InlineInspector 3.0 detects even the smallest hickeys, splashes, scratches, creases, streaks, scumming, or smearing just as reliably as it detects colour deviations or substrate defects.

InlineColorPilot 3.0 measures and controls register and colour automatically without the need to draw a sheet from the delivery. This saves time and sheet wastage, improves colour stability, and offers complete documentation of the stable production run.

Optimizes investment

The all-inclusive ProServ 360° Performance two-year programme delivers maximum return on the customers’ investment to ensure that the press runs with optimum efficiency and maximum up-time.

High-end, accessible services

With 24X7 remote service, regular inspections and maintenance, performance analysis, and direct access to the experts in Germany, Manroland also offers first-rate services.
