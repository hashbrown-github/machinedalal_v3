Müller Martini – Your Strong Partner

They provide innovative solutions for the finishing of digital and offset printed products.

As a pioneer of the graphic arts industry, Muller Martini’s top-quality print finishing systems enjoy global recognition. Muller Martini is an international, independent family business that has always identified market trends early ever since it was founded in 1946.

Highly automated machines

Muller Martini’s highly automated machines are state of the art today. They come into their own in combination with smart connectivity and a seamless workflow. Under their Finishing 4.0 development strategy, they even provide innovative solutions for the finishing of digital and offset printed products.

On-site support

With over 20 sales and service companies, 40 agencies and production sites in Switzerland, Germany, the USA, and China, Muller Martini can rapidly provide on-site support in all continents

Their services help:

  • Optimize your operating and investment costs

  • Secure your productivity, quality and added value

  • Provide reliable investment protection for your production systems

  • Maintain and increase the profitability of your equipment.

Making beginnings better

Are you planning a new project or a machine relocation? Whether you are making a new investment, restructuring or expanding your business, you can rely on the technical expertise of their sales staff, consultants, and service technicians. Their professional project management, perfect installation and commissioning of your machine ensures productivity right from the start.

Muller Martini provides you with

  • Minimum equipment downtime

  • A global logistics network

  • Quick supply of spare parts for the whole product range

  • High-quality original spare parts with a guarantee

  • Repair service on site or at Muller Martini

  • Specialists with many years of experience.

So, choose reliability, choose Muller Martini.
