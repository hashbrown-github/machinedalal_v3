The New Primera PRO

A key highlight of the 14,000-cycle machine is its variable-thickness three-knife trimmer.

The New Primera PRO adjusts itself fully automatically due to its motion control technology. So, no manual intervention is required for its setup!

This machine is at the centre of a technological leap forward from Muller Martini. And the focus is on reducing manual intervention and thereby optimizing the production process and achieving even higher-quality end products.

No need to adjust anything by hand

The completely redesigned three-knife trimmer of the Primera PRO is fully automated. With advanced motion control technology and servo drives, each of its format axes can be individually adjusted in case of any corrections – without compromise and without affecting other axes.

Its two-cut transport rollers can be individually corrected during "lift-off" – just like the two front stops, for example – without stopping the entire line.

Automation lowers costs

The targeted expansion of the motion control technology for the entire machine ensures a significant reduction in setup and throughput times for your jobs and, due to the fast and error-free setup processes, increases your profitability.

Fast learning

The all-inclusive ProServ 360° Performance two-year programme delivers maximum return on the customers’ investment to ensure that the press runs with optimum efficiency and maximum up-time.

The new interaction is therefore particularly easy to learn, which in turn reduces the need for training. This reduces your costs and is also a great advantage when changing personnel.

To sum it all, the Primera PRO, with its motion control technology, significantly shortens the setup and throughput times for jobs, thus ensuring maximum profitability.
